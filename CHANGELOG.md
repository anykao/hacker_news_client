## 0.1.0

- Initial version of a a Hacker News Client for Dart
- Works on Flutter, Server, and Web
- Covered by tests
- Supports:
  - Top Stories
  - Best Stories
  - New Stories
  - Jobs
  - Ask Hacker News
  - Show Hacker News
  - Story with all comments
  - Poll with Options and all comments
  - User
